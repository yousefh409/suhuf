## Sheikh Instagram Strategy

The Sheikh's Instagram following is the highest-leverage organic channel. His audience already trusts him on Arabic and Islamic education — that trust transfers directly to i3rab. One authentic Reel from him does more than weeks of cold outreach.

### Why It Works

- **Trust shortcut**: Arabic learners won't trust an AI to judge their grammar unless someone credible vouches for it. A scholar demonstrating the app live removes that hesitation instantly.
- **Built-in shareability**: Muslim educational content gets shared in DMs, stories, and WhatsApp groups as sadaqah jariyah. Every Reel has a second life off-platform.
- **Right demographic**: His followers are already studying Arabic or interested in Islamic sciences — no wasted impressions.

### Content Formats


| Format                                      | Description                                                                                                     | When                                               |
| ------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| "Can AI catch MY mistakes?"                 | Sheikh reads a text, app flags an error in real-time. Genuine reaction. Educational + entertaining.             | Phase 1 (beta) — builds anticipation before launch |
| "Building something for students of Arabic" | Behind-the-scenes: showing the app in development, asking followers what texts they want, what features matter. | Phase 1 — creates a waitlist and early buy-in      |
| "Grammar tip of the day"                    | Uses i3rab as the visual aid to explain a common nahw mistake (e.g., confusing marfu' and mansub).              | Phase 2 onward — recurring series, 2-3x/week       |
| "Student challenge"                         | "Read this hadith into i3rab and post your screenshot." Drives downloads directly through participation.        | Phase 2 launch week — spike in installs            |
| "I tested my own students"                  | Shows students using the app in a halaqah, their reactions when AI catches mistakes. Relatable, funny.          | Phase 3 — social proof from real usage             |
